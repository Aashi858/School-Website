Modern Delhi Public School, Faridabad

About

Under the dynamic leadership and exemplary vision of an eminent educationist and its co-promoter Mr. U.S.Verma, (Founder Principal D.P.S, Faridabad and Former Vice Principal, D.P.S, R.K Puram), Modern Delhi Public School, Faridabad is another ambitious effort at setting up one more educational paradigm, to meet the aspirations of students and offer Teachers a rich and Commensurate transactional environment. The school is sprawled over 10 acres of land and is designed to provide a feeling of space, air and light. It offers state-of-the-art infrastructure to its students.The guiding principle of Modern Delhi Public School is provision for a total and meaningful education and thus prepare our young ones to face the modern day challenges of the competitive world. The primary focus is on skill-building and personality development rather than textual literacy. Our main objective is to inculcate and develop vital life skills such as communication, creativity, management, leadership and decision making, to condition them to develop poise and presence to give shape to the nation.

School acheivements

Phenomenal success in NEET 2020
Phenomenal success in NEET 2020

In the words of renowned philosopher Aristotle, "Excellence is never an accident. It is the result of high intention, sincere effort and intelligent execution; choice, not chance, determines your destiny." The glory of the diligent minds of Modern Delhi Public School reached a new high with their outstanding performance in NEET 2020. Both Samriddhi Khare and Ajab Singh have brought laurels to the institution by securing AIR 1072(Cat. Rank 683) and AIR 1073(Cat. Rank 302) respectively. With 100% students qualifying NEET this year, the school's academic prowess stands unparalleled. Mr. Udai Verma, the Director Principal, congratulated the star performers on their stupendous success and wished them the best for their bright futures ahead.

Delhi University JAT-2020
Delhi University JAT-2020

Achieving new heights of excellence, the dynamic students of Modern Delhi Public School brought laurels galore with their top notch performance in the competitive exams. Saksham Gambhir and Gaurang M Lakhani upped the ante by securing AIR 27 and AIR 100 in Delhi University JAT-2020. Lauding their brilliant feat, Mr. Udai Verma, the Director Principal, congratulated the star performers on their trailblazing success and encouraged all the students to be inspired by the concerted efforts and unwavering determination of the young role models.

CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) 2020
CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) 2020

Carrying forward the rich legacy of Modern Delhi Public School, Bhavya Adhana, a student of the academic year 2019-20, secured the AIR 129 in CLAT (Common Law Admission Test). Adding yet another feather in the adorned crown of the school, Bhavya is The State Topper. Having secured the first rank in the state of Haryana, Bhavya's exemplary dedication and commitment towards his goal will continue to aspire generations of young aspirants. Mr. Udai Verma, the Director Principal, congratulated Bhavya, his parents and the teachers on the remarkable feat and wished him the very best for his bright future ahead.
