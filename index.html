Modern Delhi Public School, Faridabad

About

Under the dynamic leadership and exemplary vision of an eminent educationist and its co-promoter Mr. U.S.Verma, (Founder Principal D.P.S, Faridabad and Former Vice Principal, D.P.S, R.K Puram), Modern Delhi Public School, Faridabad is another ambitious effort at setting up one more educational paradigm, to meet the aspirations of students and offer Teachers a rich and Commensurate transactional environment. The school is sprawled over 10 acres of land and is designed to provide a feeling of space, air and light. It offers state-of-the-art infrastructure to its students.The guiding principle of Modern Delhi Public School is provision for a total and meaningful education and thus prepare our young ones to face the modern day challenges of the competitive world. The primary focus is on skill-building and personality development rather than textual literacy. Our main objective is to inculcate and develop vital life skills such as communication, creativity, management, leadership and decision making, to condition them to develop poise and presence to give shape to the nation.
